Addressing memory problems by recognizing friends' faces

Concerning Controversy

What's Your Name addresses the controversy behind facial recognition and the expansion of integrated cameras. How can recognition software respect privacy and still be used in meaningful ways? What's Your Name only operates within private systems that the user has been given access to, such as a Facebook friends list, or publicly available registries, such as the National Sex Offender Registry.

Social Benefit

What's Your Name has the ability to take from public records of persons, including sex offenders and police officers to provide real time, real world benefit including identification of predators or suspicious persons, and police officers in live conflict.

Treating Illness

We believe What's Your Name will provide victims of alzheimer's disease a tool to support and encourage recognition of family and friends. Through a simple gesture, patients can have a full name and pictures associated with memories of the person in front of them appear on the transparent screen that Google Glass provides.
"Okay Glass, recognize this?"
